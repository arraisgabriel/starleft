## Villains
Villains are bosses that have their own map, serve as knowledge/ability gate for the next episodes and have custom rendering using neon glows maped to their sprites (Take inspiration in HUBs building sprites, glowing animations generation and game wiring) so they can appear beautifull on screen.

They also have really BIG sizes that vary between them. The first one, the cyan blue ninja from episode 7.5 (new villain map) is 2 times bigger than Nino. Rex, the black and green second boss from A&O in episode 15, is HUGE mech 5 times bigger than Nino. 

They have special abilities like heroes do. Suggest to balance them well. The ninja MUST be very very fast so it can leave the visible area quickly when he is almost dying. The mech MUST be reeeeaally strong, have a reeeeeeally big life bar and get in Madosis state, becoming more agressive, in his second phase. 

Villains have a special hp life bar animation that is visible througout all map in the HUD.

Plan on how to create the villains system and wire the two first villains and their maps to the game.

## Veteran enemies 

Veteran enemies are randomly carried over enemies. Like veteran units, veteran enemies are carried over a player's campaign respecting a certain limit in each episode. Each nemesis has their own personality and will rise or fall within their social structure as the game progresses. Their progress is affected by their actions in-game, and each will react differently. By defeating these notable enemies, the player is rewarded with extra m3rits.

Veteran enemies are a exact copy ov veteran units carried over by the player. Balance them throught the campaign accordingly.
