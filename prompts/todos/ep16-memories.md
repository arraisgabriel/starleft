This map introduces a new mechanic that is: Memory extraction from chips inside the head of dead people (civilians and units).

One of the objectives here is to get the memories of 5 dead bodies that are spread across the map stripe, each one with a paragraph long dialog summarizing the last hours of the body.

To get the memories from a dead body chip the player MUST command a hero to approach the body and get it (clicking in the body).

The last memory MUST ALWAYS be the "Dell Tusk has been a cyborg for many years".
